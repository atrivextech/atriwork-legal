Delete Your Account & Data

AtriWork · by AtriVex Technology

This page explains how to request deletion of your AtriWork account and the data associated with it. AtriWork is a worker attendance and payroll app published by AtriVex Technology.

Most of your data is on your device

AtriWork stores your workers, attendance, payroll, and worker face templates locally on your phone. To remove all local data, simply uninstall the app, or clear its storage from Android Settings → Apps → AtriWork → Storage → Clear data. You can also delete individual workers, attendance, or payroll records inside the app at any time.

Deleting your account and cloud data

If you signed in with Google or use a paid plan (Basic / Pro) with Cloud Sync or Google Drive backup, some data (your account identifier, subscription entitlement, and — for Pro — synced workers, attendance, and payroll) is stored in our secure cloud or your own Google Drive. To request deletion of your account and this cloud data:

  1. Email support@atrivextech.com from the email address you used to sign in.
  2. Use the subject line “Delete my AtriWork account and data”.
  3. We verify the request and delete your account and associated cloud data within 30 days.

You may also delete the AtriWork backup directly from your own Google Drive at any time.
