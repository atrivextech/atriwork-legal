About AtriWork

AtriWork helps factory owners and supervisors manage their workforce from one phone — mark attendance with face recognition or manual entry, calculate weekly payroll with overtime and advances, share payslips, and back up to your own Google Drive. AtriWork works offline and keeps your data on your device by default.
